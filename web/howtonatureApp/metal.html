How to discharge Metal exactly?

Remove foreign substances such as emptying the contents and rinsing them with water.

Remove metal cans and other materials such as plastic lids and drain them.

Scrap metal such as driver, plier, spanner, wrench, hammer, saw, wire and nail and non-scrap metal like aluminum and stainless steel not mixed and then put in an envelope or tied with a string to discharge them.

NOTICE

Butane gas or insecticide containers are completely removed and discharged, such as drilling holes or pressing nozzles in a well-ventilated place.
