How to discharge Paper exactly?

Remove tape and other materials from the box and fold it out so that foreign substances are not mixed.

Keep newspapers from getting wet, spread them evenly, stack them one by one, and tie them up so that vinyl-coated advertisements and vinyls are not mixed.

Remove the plastic coated cover and the spring of the notebook and discharge it out.

NOTICE

Empty the contents of a paper pack or a paper cup, rinse it with water once, press it to remove other materials such as straws and vinyl, and then put them in an envelope or tie them together to discharge them.

Please discharge paper mixed with other materials, non-paper thing, and paper contaminated with oil or food such as receipts, delivery invoice, gold foil, foil in standard plastic garbage bag.
