How to discharge Plastic exactly?

Empty the contents of the PET bottle and plastic container clean, and remove parts made of other materials such as attachment marks and lids.

However, please put products that are difficult to separate, such as pill packaging and cassette tape, in a pay-as-you-go bag.

Disconnect and drain the pump for pumping containers such as shampoo and dishwasher.

If the lid is made of the same material, compress it and close the lid again to discharge it.

NOTICE

Plastic is divided into different types depending on the material.

Among them, plastic OTHER is a composite plastic mixed with various materials according to the purpose of the product and cannot be recycled unless it undergoes a separate recycling process. So they can't be selected from recycling sites, and most of them are disposed of as general waste (incinerated or buried).

Because the material itself is plastic, government guidelines indicate plastic separation emissions rather than regular waste. However, there is a need to recognize that recycle is not practically feasible and reduce the use of plastic OTHER materials as much as possible.
